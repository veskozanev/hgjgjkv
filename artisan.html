Artisan: A Direct-to-customer e-Commerce Platform

Introducing Artisan, a personal UX/UI design project focused on delivering a direct-to-customer platform for effortlessly customising gift hampers and delivering it to clients. This innovative service streamlines the process of creating tailor-made gift baskets for relatives or close friends, ensuring a delightful gifting experience.


Project Details

Industry: e-Commerse, Direct-to-customer

Project duration: 2 weeks

Deliverable: Create a direct-to-customer platform that is solving a problem

Design Team: Iliyana Pirinska

My responsibilities

  • UX/UI Design Research

  • Visual Identity

  • UX/UI Design

Initial Project idea

As a part of an online course accesment, I had to create a design for a service that solves a problem. The initial concept for the project was centered around the idea of developing a straightforward and user-friendly platform that enables users to effortlessly customize gift hampers for their relatives or friends. To gain a deeper understanding of user needs and preferences, I decided to create a questionnaire, which would help gather valuable insights into the users' gifting habits, product interests, and desired personalization options. This data-driven approach allowed me to design a more targeted and efficient platform, ultimately ensuring that the gift customization process is as simple and enjoyable as possible for users.

Questionnaire

After carefully analyzing the responses from the questionnaire, I identified the perceived advantages and drawbacks expressed by users. This valuable feedback informed the development of my goals and guiding principles for the project. Additionally, it provided insights into potential product expansion opportunities and the ideal niche for a service of this nature to thrive. The insights were gathered from 48 participants, ensuring a well-rounded perspective on user needs and preferences.
