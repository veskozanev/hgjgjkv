About Me

I am a UX/UI designer based in Edinburgh, Scotland. I am focused on creating clean and intuitive interfaces across all platforms and devices. My design philosophy is centred around empathy, usability, and accessibility. I am dedicated to create designs that are aesthetically pleasing, functional and make a positive impact in people's lives. I am interested in freelancing for challenging UX/UI projects with a human-centred mission.

Currently, I work as a UX/UI Design Analyst at Syrona Health - a digital healthcare company. My crucial responsibilities are ensuring a smooth user experience, conducting comprehensive usability testing and creating intuitive design through data-driven innovative solutions for an m-Health app - SORA. Designing for digital healthcare taught me the importance of human-centred design and why designers should always consider accessibility, empathy, usability and equity.

Alongside working, I am a fourth-year undergraduate student studying Web Design and Development at Edinburgh Napier University, on track to achieve a first-class degree. Throughout my studies, I built a solid foundational understanding of front-end development, business analysis, marketing, user research and web design. My diverse background allows me to make informed decisions quickly, benefiting both users and business strategies.

When I'm not immersed in solving UX/UI problems or designing, you can find me at sports centres playing volleyball, pushing myself at the gym during cardio classes, or seeking out beautiful landscapes to capture with my camera.

As a designer, my goal is to be both compassionate and well-informed, advocating for the needs of users, striving to design for a better world. I am inspired by the words of Mahatma Gandhi: "Be the change you want to see in the world," and I strive to embody this philosophy in both my personal and professional life.

My values

01 / Lifelong learner

Learning is a lifelong journey, and I embrace it with open arms. As a UX/UI designer, I am constantly seeking out new insights and knowledge in the field, through attending conferences, reading industry publications, and participating in design forums. I believe that staying informed and educated is essential to providing the best solutions for my clients.

02 / Quality over quantity

I believe that quality is the hallmark of great design. As a UX/UI designer, I strive to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also functional, intuitive, and user-friendly. I believe that by focusing on quality over quantity, I can create designs that truly resonate with users and provide a meaningful experience.

03 / Stay humble and attentive to detail

I strongly believe in the importance of humility in design, which allows us to remain receptive to feedback and continuously learn from others. I'm passionate about improving my work and devote close attention to every detail, with a focus on delivering high-quality results. Regardless of the project's size or complexity, I believe that it deserves the same level of care and dedication to ensure that the final design meets the user's needs and exceeds expectations.
